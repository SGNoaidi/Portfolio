Devils Nite & The Art Show (a digital experiment)

My focus here was accessibility and redistribution. I hosted both of these events with B.I.M.B.O.S. which at the time was a group of sex workers from all over the US. Our motivation was to give financial relief during covid. Both events were private and "ticket gated" so there is not a lot of photos out of respect for the artists.
Both of these events went very well and each made around $5k.

    The first show was Devils Night where each member did halloween performance art.

      The second event, "The Art Show", was a collection of digital art from 10 different artists/sexworkers across the US.
